About Us

Welcome to NeoCode, a program created by Fundación dos in collaboration with the National University of Engineering (UNI). Inspired by the "NeoCode" course at the University of Cambridge, our goal is to provide an introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming.

At NeoCode, we believe that mastering the code is the key to conquering the world. We strive to provide a dynamic and enriching educational environment where students can explore the fundamentals of computer science and develop essential programming skills. With our tagline "Master the Code, Conquer the World," we aim to empower individuals to harness the power of technology and create a better future.

Join us on the exciting journey of discovery and learning in the world of computer science. Together, we can explore the endless possibilities offered by computer science and the art of programming.

Welcome to NeoCode, where mastering the code is the first step to conquering the world!

Most asked questions

What is NeoCode?

NeoCode is a program by Fundación dos in collaboration with the National University of Engineering (UNI), based on the NeoCode course from Cambridge University, titled "Introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming."

How do i access to the course?

To enroll in this couse you have to vist our instalations, contact us for more info. The course includes virtual sessions that you can attend from home or any location with internet access, as well as in-person sessions with laboratory practices, tutors, and plenty of fun. The in-person sessions take place at the National University of Engineering in Managua, with flexible scheduling, or remotely via virtual attendance.

What do i need?

The course is free of charge for you; however, we require regular attendance at lectures, virtual sessions, and other in-person activities. To successfully complete the course, approximately 14 hours per week are needed, including time for in-person sessions, virtual attendance, study hours, and work at home. It is preferable to have a computer with internet access and to submit assignments or PSets promptly. With dedication and effort, we are confident you can succeed.
